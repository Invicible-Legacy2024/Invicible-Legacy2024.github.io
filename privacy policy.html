Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not just important; it's sacred. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Who We Are

BlockchainMall.org is a luxury-grade e-commerce ecosystem that connects buyers and sellers in a secure, transparent, and decentralized environment. Powered by blockchain, we ensure unmatched data integrity, trust, and privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of data:

Personal Information (PI)

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Shipping & Billing Address
  • Payment Information (via secure processors)
  • Account Login Credentials

Technical & Behavioral Data

  • IP Address
  • Device Information
  • Browser Type
  • Location (if permitted)
  • Shopping behavior
  • Blockchain wallet addresses (optional)

3. How We Collect It

  • When you register or sign in
  • During checkout or order processing
  • Via cookies, pixels, and analytics tools
  • Through your interactions with merchants and support

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Fulfill and manage orders
  • Verify user and merchant accounts
  • Secure transactions via blockchain integration
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Offer personalized product recommendations
  • Send important updates, receipts, and promotions
  • Combat fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

5. Data Protection & Security

We use military-grade encryption, blockchain immutability, and zero-knowledge proofs (where applicable) to safeguard your data. All transactions are secured via SSL/TLS, and sensitive data is never stored in plain text.

Our servers are hosted in Tier IV secure environments and are GDPR, CCPA, and ISO 27001 compliant.

6. Your Rights

As a valued user, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your data
  • Update or correct personal details
  • Request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent to marketing
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Report misuse or breaches directly to our DPO

To exercise any of the above, email: privacy@blockchainmall.org

7. Third-Party Integrations

We only share data with trusted services necessary for smooth operation, such as payment gateways (Stripe, CryptoPay), analytics (Google Analytics), and fraud prevention tools.

They are contractually bound to protect your data and never sell it.

8. Cookies & Smart Tracking

We use cookies and blockchain-friendly tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze traffic and performance
  • Serve personalized offers

You may manage your cookie preferences in your browser or opt out anytime via our Cookie Settings page.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

If you suspect any violation, contact: legal@blockchainmall.org

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology, law, or our practices. Major changes will be announced via email or dashboard alert.
